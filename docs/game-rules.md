# Anime Arcana Exchange — Game Rules (v2, post-playtest)

## Overview
2-4 players move around a 22-tile board, collecting anime-themed cards, earning income, and navigating events. The player with the highest score at game end wins.

## Setup
- Each player starts with **10 coins** and **1 random card**
- Hand limit: **8 cards** maximum
- Players start at tile 0 (START)

## Turn Structure
1. **Roll & Move** — Roll d6, move that many tiles clockwise
2. **Execute Tile Effect** — Perform action on landed tile
3. **Hand Limit Check** — If >8 cards, must sell or discard excess
4. **End Turn** — Pass to next player. **Pass-and-play handoff** screen blocks the device until the next player taps "Reveal" so cards remain hidden.

## Board Tiles (22 total)
| Tile | Effect |
|------|--------|
| START | Collect income (sum of all card incomes) + draw Hype Event |
| BUY PACK | Buy 1 card from deck (3 coins) |
| SELL CARDS | Sell up to 2 cards at fixed prices |
| TAX | Pay 5 coins OR discard 1 card |
| RAID ZONE | Roll d6: 1-3 lose 3 coins, 4-5 lose 1 card, 6 nothing |
| LUCKY DRAW (Chance) | Draw from Chance deck |
| AUCTION | **Sealed-bid** — all players write a bid privately, highest wins and pays their bid |
| BLACK MARKET | Choose: +4 coins OR take 1 card then discard 1 |
| INSURANCE OFFICE | Pay 3 coins — blocks **one** Raid loss (one-shot, clarified) |

> Note: the original "Parking" tile was a dead square; v2 replaces it with a **Lucky Draw** for a positive moment.

## Card System
### Collections
- **Shadow Sorcery** (Jujutsu-inspired) — 10 cards
- **Shinobi Legends** (Naruto-inspired) — 10 cards
- **Bizarre Arcana** (JoJo-inspired) — 10 cards

### Rarities
| Rarity | Income | Sell Price | Per Collection |
|--------|--------|-----------|----------------|
| Common | +1 | 2 | 5 cards |
| Rare | +2 | 3 | 3 cards |
| Epic | +4 | 6 | 1 card |
| Legendary | +6 | 10 | 1 card |

### Special Abilities
- **Leader** — Income ×1.5 (rounded). Sell value also gains the bonus.
- **Stability** — +1 income while a Hype Event is active

### Legendary Gate (v2)
Legendary cards cannot appear in BUY PACK until **at least one player has passed START once**. Removes the "first-pull-wins" snowball.

## Chance Deck (v2)
- +3 coins (×2)
- +2 coins (×1)
- −3 coins (×1)
- −2 coins (×1)
- Take 1 **Common/Rare** card from an opponent
- Give 1 **Common/Rare** card to an opponent

> Epics and Legendaries are **immune** to Chance card transfers (v2 fix).

## Hype Events
Drawn when passing START. Buffs one collection (+1 income), nerfs another (−1 income). Card income never goes below 0.

### Catch-Up (v2)
A player who is **15+ coins behind** the leader, when passing START, draws **2 Hype Events and chooses 1**. The unchosen event returns to the queue.

## End Condition (v2)
Game ends when the **first player passes START for the 4th time** (was 6 in v1). All remaining players finish the current round.

## Scoring
**Final Score = Total Coins + Sum of All Card Sell Prices** (Leader bonus included).

Highest score wins. Ties broken by card count.

---

## Quick Reference
A `?` button in the lower-right of the game screen opens a one-page rules card with all tile effects, rarity table, and ability descriptions — useful for first-time players.
